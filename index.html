A Menstruation Guide

Who am I?

I'm a qualified nurse and mother of two girls. I understand the confusion and concerns that can arise for young pre-teens, teenagers, and young adult women regarding menstruation. That's why I've created an easy-to-understand guide to help navigate the experience, providing support, clearing up myths, and emphasizing safety and hygiene.
