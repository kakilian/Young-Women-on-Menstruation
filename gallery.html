What can I do? - self awareness

There are two main things, I have broken them down to: Non-medical and medication. Always try the non-medical things first, to see which fits your body best and when. Also which products, to-date, which are avaliable to us.

Non-Medical: Opt for warm bottles and blankets, keep hydrated by drinking water, cut back on salt, alcohol, and caffeine intake, indulge in warm baths, prioritize rest, and contemplate incorporating supplements such as calcium, Vitamin D, and iron into your diet.

Medical: Over-the-counter anti-inflammatory medications like Naproxen or Ibuprofen may alleviate symptoms, but it's important to use them under adult supervision or guidance from a medical professional.

Materials for Hygiene Management: Choose from sanitary pads, menstrual underwear, or tampons based on your needs and comfort level. Make sure to dispose of products properly and change them regularly.
